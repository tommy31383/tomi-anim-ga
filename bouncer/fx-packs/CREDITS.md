# FX Packs Bundled with Tomi Bouncer

All packs included here are **CC0** (public domain — free for commercial use, no attribution required, attribution given anyway as courtesy).

## kenney/ — Kenney Particle Pack

- **Source**: https://www.kenney.nl/assets/particle-pack
- **Author**: Kenney (https://kenney.nl)
- **License**: CC0 1.0 Universal
- **Bundled files**: 19 single-frame particle PNGs (muzzle / spark / scorch / flare / twirl) + License.txt
- **Original size**: 14MB (full pack with 80 sprites). Bundled 19 most useful for combat FX.

## blood/ — OpenGameArt Blood + Hit Animations

- **Source**: https://opengameart.org/content/animations-blood-hit-and-both-d
- **Original License**: CC0
- **Bundled files**: 3 sprite sheets (2048×2048, 4×4 grid = 16 frames each)
  - `blood-splat-1.png`
  - `blood-splat-2.png`
  - `blood-splat-3.png`
- **Frame size**: 512×512 each

## Usage in Bouncer

Sidebar → **🖼️ FX sprite custom** → click **"Load preset"** dropdown → pick a bundled pack.

Or upload your own PNG sprite sheet via drag-drop.
